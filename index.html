Welcome to my live counter!

Use the buttons on the screen to increment or decrement the counter value by 1.

Hold Shift while clicking to modify the count by 10.

You can also use your keyboard:

  • Press + or ↑ to add 1;
  • Press - or ↓ to subtract 1;
  • Hold Shift and press ↑ or ↓ to add or subtract 10.

Enjoy!
